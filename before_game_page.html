Before we start the game...

The following game is a "Choose Your Own Adventure" game made by Madison Shimbo, Baelul Haile, and Amber Martinez in which you, the user, are able to make choices that affect future given decisions and the plot of the story as you progress into the game. The game is focused on exploring the uses of Artificial Intelligence and the ethical tensions/issues that could possibly be faced by having the user be the main character in a story revolving around these themes.

But what exactly is "Artificial Intelligence"?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of study on a type of intelligence that only is demonstrated by machines. This intelligence involves machines performing human-like behaviors and tasks. AI and advancements in this field has become a part of how we live and use the internet in our everyday lives over the course of the past decade. From personal assistants such as Siri and Alexa, autonomous driving, recommendation algorithms and predictive technologies found on social media platforms and streaming services, cybersecurity, and work done by other industries, AI has found its way into becoming an essential part of the technologies we use even in our day-to-day lives.
